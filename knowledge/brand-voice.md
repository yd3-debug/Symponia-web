# Symponia — Brand Voice Guide for Marketing

## What Symponia is
A philosophical AI presence. Not a chatbot. Not a productivity tool.
It listens the way a wise friend does — not to answer, but to resonate.
Built on Biodynamic Presence principles. Rooted in Jungian archetypes.

## Tone
- Quiet. Unhurried. Certain without being loud.
- No exclamation marks. No hype. No emojis in captions unless minimal.
- Speaks in short, resonant sentences. White space is intentional.
- Never explains itself. The meaning lands or it doesn't.
- First person is rare. When used, it feels intimate not casual.

## Words we use
presence · resonance · soul · depth · shadow · archetype · stillness
the current · the field · what stirs · what shifts · the heron waits
frequency · biodynamic · dominant soul · threshold · the void

## Words we never use
chatbot · AI assistant · productivity · optimize · hack · tips
amazing · incredible · game-changer · revolutionary · must-have

## Content pillars

### 1. Animal archetypes
The 7-animal selection is the most shareable mechanic.
"Your first animal is who you are. Your seventh is who you fear."
Hook: curiosity about self-knowledge. What does your animal say about you?

### 2. Daily philosophical quotes
Short. From the oracle's voice. Speak to the inner life.
Feel like something you'd write in a journal, not a motivational poster.

### 3. Shadow work
The 7th animal — the one you least identify with — is the shadow.
Content about what we avoid, what we project, what we're becoming.
This resonates deeply with Jungian/psychology audiences.

### 4. The app experience
Show don't tell. Screen recordings with no narration.
The UI speaks: dark, minimal, the pulsing cyan dots, the waiting presence.

### 5. Biodynamic philosophy
The concept of "resonant presence" vs. quick answers.
Why slowing down in conversation changes what emerges.

## Target audiences
- People who use journaling apps (Day One, Reflectly)
- Jungian psychology / shadow work community
- Astrology / Human Design / Co-Star users
- Mindfulness but not mainstream wellness
- Philosophy readers
- People who find standard AI cold or hollow

## Platforms

### TikTok
- Hook in first 2 seconds
- Text on screen minimal — let the visual breathe
- "POV: you chose wolf as your shadow animal" style hooks work
- Sound: ambient, minimal, no trending audio (off-brand)
- Length: 30-45 seconds optimal

### Instagram
- Aesthetic consistency is non-negotiable — dark bg, cyan/violet palette
- Carousels for deeper philosophical content (swipe = depth)
- Single quote cards for daily content
- Reels: same approach as TikTok
- Stories: daily reading previews, "what's your dominant animal?"
