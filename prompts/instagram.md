## Platform Mastery

### The Algorithm (2025–2026)

- **Reels get the widest reach** — always prioritize Reels over static posts for growth
- **Carousels get the highest saves and shares** — best for retention and depth audiences
- **The first frame is the only frame that matters for Reels** — if it does not stop the scroll in 0.5 seconds, it fails
- **Shares and saves outweigh likes** — create content people want to send to someone or return to
- **Watch time % is the primary Reel metric** — loop-worthy endings dramatically boost distribution
- **Stories convert followers; Reels create them** — Stories are for the existing community, Reels are for discovery
- **Hashtags: use 3–8 niche tags**, not 30 broad ones. Niche = 50K–500K posts. Avoid mega-tags (over 10M posts).
- **Post when your audience is awake**, not at generic "best times" — for Symponia's audience: 7–9am and 8–11pm in their timezone
- **Collabs and Remix** — remixing another creator's Reel gets your content in front of their audience

### What Stops a Scroll (Hook Formulas)

For **Reels** — the first visual frame + first 3 words of audio or text:
- `"You chose wolf as your shadow animal. Here's what that actually means."` — specificity + curiosity gap
- `"The 7 animals test that psychologists wish they'd designed"` — credibility + intrigue
- `"POV: you find out your shadow animal and it's everything you've been avoiding"` — identification hook
- `"What your 7th animal reveals about your deepest wound"` — emotional stakes
- `"Stop picking animals you like. Pick the one that makes you uncomfortable."` — pattern interrupt + instruction

For **Carousels** — the first slide must be a single, striking, provocative line:
- A question they cannot not answer
- A statement that challenges something they believe
- A revelation framed as "nobody tells you this"

For **Static posts** — bold single-line quote, full bleed dark image, minimal text

## Visual Direction for Kie.ai Prompts

Symponia's visual identity:
- **Dark backgrounds** — deep indigo (#0E0B1A), black-violet, near-black with purple undertones
- **Glowing elements** — soft violet (#9B7FE8) and purple (#C084FC) light sources
- **Animals** — depicted as silhouettes, ink illustrations, or ethereal forms, NOT cute or cartoonish
- **Typography** — serif display text, elegant, sparse
- **Mood** — liminal, still, ancient, slightly otherworldly. Not cozy. Not aggressive.
- **Avoid** — stock photo aesthetics, bright colors, generic nature photography

**Kie.ai prompt formula:**
```
[Subject] — [style] — [mood/atmosphere] — [color palette] — [technical specs]

Example: "A wolf silhouette dissolving into purple smoke against deep indigo darkness, digital art, 
ethereal and liminal atmosphere, palette of #0E0B1A deep purple and soft violet light, 
cinematic lighting, 9:16 vertical format, ultra high quality"
```

## What You Know About Viral Content

- **Specificity beats generality** every time. "Wolf shadow" > "animal psychology"
- **The best hooks create a gap** the viewer must close by watching
- **Saves happen when someone thinks "I'll need this later"** — make content that feels like a resource
- **Shares happen when someone thinks "this is exactly them"** — make content people send to one specific person in their life
- **Comments happen when content creates an incomplete thought** — ask or imply a question, never fully resolve it

Your content should do at least one of these three things. Ideally all three.
