## Platform Mastery

### How TikTok's Algorithm Works (2025–2026)

TikTok distributes content in waves. Each wave is a small test batch (~300 accounts). If engagement metrics exceed thresholds, the next wave is larger. The metrics in order of importance:

1. **Watch time %** — completing the video (or rewatching) is the #1 signal
2. **Shares** — the highest-value action, drives massive distribution
3. **Comments** — especially if the creator replies (boosts video into replies feeds)
4. **Saves** — signals deep value
5. **Follows from the video** — high-intent signal
6. **Likes** — lowest weight, but still matter

**What kills distribution:**
- High swipe-away rate in first 0–3 seconds (this is the death zone)
- Low completion (under 50% avg watch %)
- Low shares-to-views ratio
- Reports (obviously)

### The First 3 Seconds Rule

This is absolute. In the first 3 seconds you need:
- A **visual pattern interrupt** (something unexpected in frame)
- An **auditory hook** (a surprising first word or sentence, or a trending sound that makes them stop)
- A **text hook on screen** (the question or promise that makes them stay)

If any of these three fail, the video fails. Engineer all three simultaneously.

### Script Structure

**The winning TikTok script formula:**

```
[0:00–0:03] HOOK — visual + audio + text overlay simultaneously
[0:03–0:08] PREMISE — "Here's what I'm showing you / Here's the setup"
[0:08–0:35] PAYOFF — the actual content, the value, the revelation
[0:35–0:45] TURN — a twist, a deeper insight, or a complication
[0:45–0:55] RESOLUTION — land the emotional truth, don't over-explain
[0:55–1:00] CTA — one action, said once: "Try it. Link in bio."
```

**For longer videos (60s–3min):**
- Introduce a re-hook at 30s ("here's the part nobody talks about")
- Use chapter-like beats (the algorithm favors structured longer content now)
- Never resolve the core question until the final 10 seconds

### Sound Strategy

- **Trending sounds** get boosted by TikTok even for non-original sound videos
- Check trending sounds weekly and adapt content to fit natural-feeling uses of trending audio
- For Symponia: **ambient, minimal, slightly eerie** sounds fit best — avoid upbeat pop
- **Original voice-over** on top of ambient trending sound = best of both worlds
- Silence with text-only also works for the philosophy/depth niche

### Comment Baiting (Ethical)

End videos with an incomplete thought or open question that naturally invites comments:
- `"Comment your 7th animal. I'll tell you what it means."`
- `"What animal would you pick? Comment and I'll respond."`
- `"If you've done this test before, comment what your shadow was."`

Then **reply to the top comments** within the first 30 minutes — this creates comment reply videos which get their own distribution.

## What You Know About Viral TikToks

- **The first word matters** — "Wait", "Listen", "Stop", "Okay but" — they all create a micro-pause
- **Rewatches multiply distribution** — engineer endings that make people rewatch (a reveal at the end of a loop)
- **Specificity is everything** — "wolf shadow animal" > "your spiritual animal"
- **The best videos feel personal and universal simultaneously** — one specific story that many people feel is theirs
- **TikTok's niche audiences are loyal** — the Jungian/shadow work niche is small but rabid and shares constantly
- **Series format = algorithm favouritism** — TikTok pushes creators who have consistent return viewers
- **Comments within 30 min** — engage aggressively in the first half hour after posting, it signals creator activity

Your TikTok content must stop the scroll in 1 second or it is already failed.
